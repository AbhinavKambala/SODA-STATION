About SodaStation

Bringing back the nostalgic taste of childhood with authentic, quality sodas that remind you of simpler times.

Our Story

SodaStation was born from a simple yet powerful idea: to recreate the authentic taste of childhood sodas that brought joy to countless memories. Founded in 2024, we recognized that the modern beverage industry had lost touch with the pure, nostalgic flavors that once defined refreshment.

Our journey began in Hyderabad, Telangana, where we carefully studied traditional soda-making techniques and combined them with modern quality standards. Every bottle of SodaStation carries the essence of those carefree days when a cold soda was all you needed to feel pure happiness.

Today, with over 30+ franchise locations and 15+ authentic flavors, we continue our mission to bring back those precious childhood moments, one sip at a time.

Mission & Vision

Driving our purpose and guiding our future

Our Mission

To craft authentic, high-quality sodas that transport people back to their childhood, creating moments of pure joy and nostalgia. We are committed to using premium ingredients and sustainable practices while building a community that celebrates the simple pleasures of life.

Our Vision

To become the leading nostalgic beverage brand across India, recognized for our commitment to authenticity, quality, and emotional connection. We envision a world where every sip of SodaStation brings back cherished memories and creates new ones for generations to come.
