Our Story

About SodaStation

Bringing back the nostalgic taste of childhood, one refreshing sip at a time. Crafted with passion, served with love.

Our Journey

SodaStation was born from a simple yet powerful idea: to recreate the authentic taste of childhood sodas that we all remember fondly. Our journey began with a passion for bringing back those precious moments when a simple goli could brighten an entire day.

Founded in the heart of Telangana, we've dedicated ourselves to crafting premium sodas using traditional recipes combined with modern quality standards. Every bottle of SodaStation carries the essence of nostalgia while meeting today's expectations for taste and quality.
